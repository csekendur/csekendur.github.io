THE MEATBALL SHOP—PRIDE

STUDIO

ART DIRECTION, DESIGN

Who doesn't love a giant rainbow with even giant-er rainbow balls? We certainly do. The Meatball Shop went all out for Pride, and we got to come along for the ride.

We designed The Meatball Shop's float for the NYC Pride March along with all of the goodies that came along with it: t-shirts, booty shorts, temporary tattoos, mini-flags etc. Together with 120 marchers, we paraded through Manhattan in our ripped T's and meatball shorts, proclaiming "equality takes balls," and having a whole lot of fun. Plus, we were nominated for "Best Use of Theme" by the NYC Pride Committee.
