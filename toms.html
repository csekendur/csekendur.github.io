TOMS "ALL EYES ON DECK" MURAL

STUDIO

LMNOP Creative

CREATIVE DIRECTION

Leigh Nelson

ART DIRECTION + DESIGN

Cari Sekendur

With LMNOP, I developed the concept and designed this bright and poppy mural for TOMS to put all eyes on the United Nations' Global Goals. Eyeballs are eye catching, and as graphic symbols eyes speak to the the overall UN Global Goal of raising awareness toward combating poverty and also the TOMS x Global Citizen partnership, where they applied their pioneering "one for one" model to eyeglasses.
