OUT EAST

STUDIO

LMNOP Creative

CREATIVE DIRECTION

Leigh Nelson

DESIGN

Cari Sekendur

As soon as you step into Out East, a restaurant in Manhattan’s East Village, you’re transported to a carefree summer oasis. Reminiscent of a Hamptons beach house, the restaurant boasts a boardwalk ceiling, a 6-ft long antique map of Long Island, and an entire wall full of East Hampton tchotchkes. Our goal was to reflect the whimsical character of the interiors through the branding. The spirit of Out East is brought to life with a casual hand-drawn logo, typography, and playful illustrations.
