ABOUT

I'm a designer and art director seeking to create visual experiences that make the complex clear and the average exceptional. I’m currently working full-time at LMNOP Creative and am available for additional freelance projects.

As a part of the LMNOP team, I’ve had the pleasure of working with a small team of talented ladies developing brand identities, packaging, websites, digital and print collateral for clients largely in the hospitality sector. In addition, I am a core contributer to brand strategy, copywriting, project management and production. Prior to this I lived in Berlin, Germany where I worked as part of the brilliant team at The Adventures Of, designing for both small startups and global icons like Viacom.

In a previous life (before studying design), I led a team in Operations and worked in Marketing at a few fast-paced startups in Berlin, New York, and Chicago.
