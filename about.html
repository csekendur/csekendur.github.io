ABOUT

Cari Sekendur is a creative director, designer, art director, illustrator, and strategist principally based in New York City and traveling frequently.

Cari applies a unique approach to each project, tailoring her process to the client's goals. Her work blends the playful with the elegant while always focusing on a strong conceptual foundation. Her approach is full-circle, taking into account each piece of the pie throughout a project, from visual design to strategy to tone of voice and beyond.

Currently she is a Principal and Creative Director at Butter Studio. Previously, she led a team in operations and cut her chops in marketing at fast-paced ecommerce companies in Berlin, New York, and Chicago.
