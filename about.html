ABOUT

I'm a designer, art director and overall conceptual thinker. With a broad background, my strength is being able to wear many hats.

I'm currently part of the team at LMNOP Creative, where I have the pleasure of collaborating with a group of talented creatives on behalf of clients largely in the hospitality sector. In addition to design and art direction, I have led brand strategy, copywriting, and production for a number of our projects.

In a previous life, I led a team in Operations and cut my chops in Marketing at fast-paced startups in Berlin and New York.
