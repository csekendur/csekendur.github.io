MO' MONEY MO' PROGRESS

WEB DESIGN

Cari Sekendur

ILLUSTRATION

Cari Sekendur, Heidi Chisholm

Part design project, part values-driven fundraising engine, my partner Laura Zax and I created Mo' Money Mo' Progress to enable people to push our shared values forward in this time of uncertainty, by giving donations rather than physical gifts. What started out has a holiday gift guide for giving, momoneymoprogress.us is now active year-round. We leveraged Laura's six years of experience in philanthropy and my graphic design skills to create Mo’ Money Mo’ Progress. Does Mom have a passion for racial justice? Then the site directs you to donate in her name to orgs like The Advancement Project. Does Uncle Jerry concern himself with LGBTQ issues? Then the site suggests causes like Southerners on New Ground. Unlike sites like Charity Navigator, Mo’ Money Mo’ Progress -- a site by and for millennials -- presents these organizations with an approachable tone, fun visuals, and engaging copy.
