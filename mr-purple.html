MR. PURPLE

STUDIO

LMNOP Creative

CREATIVE DIRECTION

Leigh Nelson, Lindsay Pope

DESIGN

Cari Sekendur

Mr. Purple, in Manhattans's Lower East Side, is set to feel like an artist's loft, capturing the eclectic spirit of this storied neighborhood. LMNOP developed a brand identity and the persona of a fictional artist, Mr. Purple, to reflect the energy of the space and bring his vibrant spirit to life. We built a palette of collage elements, using overlapping shapes that hint at the color purple and are layered in with hand-drawn line work and photographs. We applied the branding to printed collateral, neon signage, and a website.
