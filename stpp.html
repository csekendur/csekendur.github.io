SKY TERRACE + PRIVATE PARK

STUDIO

LMNOP Creative

CREATIVE DIRECTION

Leigh Nelson

DESIGN

Cari Sekendur

Oh how rare to have two private outdoor spaces in the heart of New York City! Hudson hotel is lucky enough to boast just that. Our job was to create a fresh look for these two gorgeous lounges. Playing off of their amazing location, just steps from Central Park, we created malleable patterns made up of elements one might find in our city's great park or on a Manhattan rooftop terrace. The playful shapes reflect the quirk of Hudson, which, designed by daredevil architect Philippe Starck, is a creative escape for the design-freak within all of us.
